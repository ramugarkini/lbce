Nutrition Information

Learn about the essential nutrients your body needs and how to achieve a balanced diet. Understanding macronutrients (carbohydrates, proteins, fats) and micronutrients (vitamins and minerals) is key to maintaining overall health.

  • Carbohydrates: Provide energy. Found in grains, fruits, and vegetables.
  • Proteins: Essential for muscle repair. Found in meat, beans, and nuts.
  • Fats: Important for brain health. Found in avocados, nuts, and fish.
  • Vitamins and Minerals: Support various body functions. Found in a variety of fruits and vegetables.
